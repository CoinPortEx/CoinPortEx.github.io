# CoinPort's Sanctions Compliance Obligations  

As a licensed Australian cryptocurrency exchange, **CoinPort** is committed to adhering to international sanctions regulations. To comply with both **U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)** and **United Nations sanctions**, we are prohibited from providing services to individuals or entities in sanctioned jurisdictions.  

## Our Compliance Measures  
- **Geographic Restrictions:** We block users from accessing our platform if their IP address originates from a sanctioned country.  
- **Identity Verification:** Strict **KYC (Know Your Customer)** checks ensure we do not onboard users from prohibited regions.  
- **Ongoing Monitoring:** We continuously screen transactions to prevent unauthorized access by sanctioned parties.  

## List of Sanctioned Countries (as of 2025)  
CoinPort restricts services to users from the following countries under **U.S. and UN sanctions**:  

- **Iran**  
- **North Korea**  
- **Syria**  
- **Cuba**  
- **Russia** (certain regions and entities)  
- **Sudan**  
- **Crimea Region** (Ukraine, annexed by Russia)  
- **Venezuela** (specific government-related entities)  

For the latest updates on sanctions, refer to:  
- [U.S. Department of the Treasury - Sanctions Programs](https://home.treasury.gov/policy-issues/financial-sanctions/sanctions-programs-and-country-information)  
- [United Nations Security Council Sanctions](https://www.un.org/securitycouncil/sanctions/information)  

**CoinPort remains vigilant in upholding compliance to ensure a secure and lawful trading environment.**
